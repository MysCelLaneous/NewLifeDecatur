Meet Our Pastor

Elder Timothy D. Shelley

Elder Timothy D. Shelley serves as Pastor of New Life Church of God with a heart for biblical teaching, discipleship, worship, family, and community outreach.

His desire is to see lives transformed through the power of Jesus Christ and to help people grow in faith, purpose, and new life.

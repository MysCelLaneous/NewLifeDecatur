About Us

Faith. Family. Fellowship.

Welcome to New Life Church of God! We are a church family committed to sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ, growing together in faith, and serving our community with love.

Whether you are visiting for the first time or searching for a church home, we invite you to worship, connect, and grow with us.

Meet The Pastor

Elder Timothy D. Shelley

Elder Timothy D. Shelley serves as Pastor of New Life Church of God with a passion for biblical teaching, discipleship, and community outreach. His desire is to see lives transformed through the power of Jesus Christ.

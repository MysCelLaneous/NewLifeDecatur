ABOUT US

Welcome Home

New Life Church of God is a Christ-centered church located in Decatur, Illinois. We are committed to worship, discipleship, biblical teaching, community outreach, and helping people experience the transforming power of Jesus Christ.

Whether you're looking for a church home, returning to faith, or simply searching for answers, there's a place for you at New Life.

MEET THE PASTOR

Elder Timothy D. Shelley

Elder Timothy D. Shelley serves as Pastor of New Life Church of God with a heart for teaching God's Word, building strong families, and serving the Decatur community. His desire is to see lives transformed through faith in Jesus Christ.

About Us

Faith that grows. Family that cares. Worship that matters.

New Life Church of God is a Christ-centered church family located in Decatur, Illinois. We are committed to worship, biblical teaching, discipleship, prayer, outreach, and helping people experience new life through Jesus Christ.

Whether you are new to church, returning to faith, or looking for a place to grow, there is room for you at New Life.

Meet The Pastor

Elder Timothy D. Shelley

Elder Timothy D. Shelley serves as Pastor of New Life Church of God with a heart for teaching God’s Word, strengthening families, building community, and seeing lives transformed through the power of Jesus Christ.
